Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Summary: QuickTranslate takes your privacy seriously. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties. Your translation requests are processed solely to provide the translation service.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how QuickTranslate Chrome browser extension ("Extension", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects user data. By using the Extension, you agree to this policy.

QuickTranslate is developed and managed by SlimPDF ("Company").

2. Data Collection

2.1 Translation Data

During the translation process, the text you select is sent to third-party translation APIs (such as MyMemory) to provide the service. This data:

  • Is used solely for translation purposes
  • Is not stored on our servers
  • Transmission ends once the translation is complete

2.2 Local Storage

The following data is stored only in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and is never sent to any server:

  • Settings: Language preferences, theme selection, and feature toggles
  • Translation History: Your recent translations (optional and can be disabled)
  • Vocabulary Vault: Saved words, synonyms, and Spaced Repetition (SRS) data
  • Highlighter Data: Text highlights on web pages, stored locally per URL
  • Statistics: Total translation counts and weekly progress data
  • API Keys: OpenAI and DeepL API keys you provide are stored encrypted locally

Important: All of this data is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services and APIs

Our extension uses various trusted third-party APIs. Data is sent to these services only when the specific feature is used:

3.1 Translation Services (MyMemory & DeepL)

Default translations are performed via the MyMemory API. If you provide your own DeepL API key, translations are sent directly to DeepL. Your API key remains local and encrypted.

3.2 AI Summaries (OpenAI)

If you use the "Summarize (AI)" feature with your own OpenAI API key, selected texts are sent to OpenAI for processing. Your key is stored locally.

3.3 PDF and Image Translation (OCR.space)

When translating text from images or PDFs, the data is sent to the OCR.space API for text recognition. Images are deleted from their servers immediately after processing.

3.4 YouTube Subtitle Data

The YouTube Dual Subtitle feature captures the active subtitle segments and translates them via your chosen API. No data is stored on our servers.

3.5 Licensing (Firebase)

To activate Pro features, your email address and license key are stored in the Google Firebase Firestore database to verify your license status. This data is used solely for license management.

3.6 Payments (Paddle)

Payments are handled securely by Paddle. We never see or store your credit card or financial information. Paddle manages transaction security under its own privacy policy.

4. Refund and License Policy

Refund Terms: QuickTranslate Pro is a digitally delivered product. Due to its nature, once a license key has been delivered and activated on at least one (1) device, no refunds are accepted. However, if the license key fails to work due to a technical issue, you are entitled to a full refund within 7 days of purchase.

  • Device Limit: Each license key can be used on a maximum of 2 (two) different devices simultaneously.
  • Ownership: Purchased licenses are lifetime and require only a one-time payment.

5. Permissions

The Extension uses the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To detect text selection on the active page
  • storage: To save settings and vocabulary locally
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click translation and reading options
  • tts: For the Text-to-Speech feature
  • notifications: For vocabulary reminders and Pomodoro alerts

6. User Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to access, delete, or export your data. Since all data is local, you can manage this directly within the extension settings.
